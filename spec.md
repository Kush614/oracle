# Oracle — Attested Resolution Infrastructure for Prediction Markets

## 1. Executive Summary

**One line**

Oracle is a permissioned, adversarial resolution layer for prediction markets that gathers evidence, challenges itself, and publishes a cryptographically auditable `cited.md` file before any settlement occurs.

**Short thesis**

Prediction markets are only as trustworthy as their resolution. The status quo (UMA Optimistic Oracle, social consensus, manual determination) is adversarial, slow, and opaque. Oracle replaces it with a five-agent tournament: evidence is gathered, challenged, scored, attested, and published — all autonomously, with every step auditable after the fact.

**Product framing**

Oracle is not a prediction market app. Oracle is the trust layer prediction markets do not have.
